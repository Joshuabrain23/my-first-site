About Us

Enable Ease shines as a beacon of hope and empowerment for individuals with disabilities across Melbourne, Australia. As a trusted, registered provider under the National Disability Insurance Scheme (NDIS), Enable Ease is committed to delivering tailored, high-quality services that help participants live meaningful and independent lives.

Our Services

Daily Living Support

Comprehensive assistance with personal hygiene, domestic tasks, gardening, and transportation to nurture independence and enhance quality of life.

Accommodation Services

Variety of living arrangements including SIL, group homes, and residential care, offering safe, nurturing environments suited to individual preferences.

Community Participation

Opportunities for social engagement, recreational activities, volunteering, and skill-building programs that foster inclusion and promote development.

Personal Care

Compassionate support with daily personal tasks, medication management, mobility assistance, and specialized care to uphold dignity and health.

Travel and Transport Assistance

Reliable help with attending appointments, community activities, and travel training to ensure greater freedom and mobility.

Our Commitment

We deliver exceptional, personalized support. Our team collaborates closely with participants to craft individualized plans reflecting needs, aspirations, and cultural preferences. We ensure outstanding care with a focus on continuous improvement and NDIS standards.

Diversity & Accessibility

We offer services in English, Dari, Pashto, Turkish, Spanish, Hindi, Portuguese, and Urdu. Telehealth services are also available for convenient, secure access across Australia.

Conclusion

Enable Ease Care embodies true empowerment through compassionate, person-centered support. We empower individuals with disabilities to navigate their NDIS journey with confidence, independence, and dignity.
